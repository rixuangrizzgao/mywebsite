Elemental Analysis

CHNS FlashSmart Elemental Analyzer

The analyzer quantifies carbon, hydrogen, nitrogen, and sulfur through high-temperature catalytic flash combustion in an oxygen-rich atmosphere, quantitative reduction and chromatographic separation of the resultant gases (CO₂, H₂O, N₂, SO₂), and precise determination by integrated thermal-conductivity detection (TCD).

  • Materials: Standards (BBOT), Analytical balance (0.000 mg), Tin capsule
  • Gas supply: “carrier gas” helium and “reduction gas” oxygen
